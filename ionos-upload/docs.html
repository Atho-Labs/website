Active Baseline Snapshot

  • Chain model Payment-focused public/private UTXO with proof of work and one active public address class.
  • Units and fees 1 ATHO = 100,000 atoms, fee floor 1 atom/vB, minimum tx fee 500 atoms.
  • Confirmations Standard and private confirmations are both 6; coinbase maturity is 150.
  • Supply policy Max supply is 1,000,000,000.00000 ATHO with no premine and a single miner-fee path.
